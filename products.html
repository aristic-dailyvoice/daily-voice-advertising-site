Things People Ask Us

Can I run more than one product at the same time?
You can run as many products as you'd like. A lot of advertisers mix and match based on their goals, combining a Branded Article for long-term visibility with an Email Blast for an immediate push, for example. We'll help you figure out what makes the most sense for your budget and timeline.
How quickly can my campaign go live?
Once we have everything we need from you, we can typically get your campaign live within 1 business day. If you have a specific date in mind for a sale or event, just let us know and we'll plan around it.
How do I know which product is right for my budget?
We work with businesses of all sizes. Just reach out and tell us a bit about what you're trying to accomplish and we'll put together a recommendation that fits your goals and your budget, with no pressure and no commitment.
Do I need to provide the content or creative, or do you help with that?
You provide the content or creative and we take care of the rest. That said, we do offer creative services for advertisers who need help putting something together, particularly for longer campaigns. Just ask and we'll let you know what's available.
Do you offer packages for local businesses?
Yes. We offer a range of packages tailored to different goals and budgets. Whether you're looking to build awareness, drive traffic, or promote a specific offer, there's likely a package that fits. Reach out and we'll walk you through the options.
Can I target just my town or a specific area?
Yes. All of our products can be targeted by town, county, or state. You choose exactly which Daily Voice markets you want to reach, so you're never paying to advertise outside your service area.
Can I target areas beyond my town?
Absolutely. You can target multiple towns, a full county, or an entire state. Daily Voice reaches communities across a wide network, so if your business serves a broader area we can make sure your campaign covers it.
How do I get started?
The easiest way is to fill out our contact form and someone from our team will get back to you shortly. You can also email us directly at advertisers@dailyvoice.com.
